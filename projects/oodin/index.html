OODIN

OODIN is an eyewear brand with origins in Gothenburg, Sweden. In 2016 I established a graphic profile for their products and marketing, and built their online store.

The company name derives from 'Odin', a god in Norse mythology who's accompanied by his pair of ravens Huginn and Muninn. The logotype is inspired by this story, featuring a stylized pair of ravens. We used this symbol on the frames of the sunglasses, and we also created a wordmark that can be used independently from the symbol, as seen on the online store.
