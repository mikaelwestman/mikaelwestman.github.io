OODIN

In 2016 I established the brand guidelines and built the online store for OODIN, an eyewear brand with origins in Gothenburg, Sweden.

The company name derives from 'Odin', the god in Norse mythology who's accompanied by his pair of ravens. We used the ravens as inspiration for the logotype, creating a symbol featuring two stylized birds. In addition to the symbol we created a wordmark that can be used independently from the symbol.
