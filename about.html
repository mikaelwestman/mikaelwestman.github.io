I'm a designer with a love for crafting and shipping digital products. Building things together with others—concepting, iterating, and obsessing the details before releasing a finished product to the world is what brings me joy professionally.

Good user experiences are built from the fundamentals: do the research, work iteratively, let form follow function. But in all that work I strive to not lose sight of the lovable details that add character to digital products; beautiful typography, icons, illustrations, and microinteractions.

Outside of work I often pursue other creative outlets; music production, 3D modeling, painting, or cooking. And sometimes I just sit on the couch contemplating life while watching a football game.
