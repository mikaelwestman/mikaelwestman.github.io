Bio

Hello,

I'm Mikael Westman, a Swedish designer based in Stockholm. After a decade in tech, I stepped away from screens to work in three dimensions, making furniture and objects for the physical world.

It's been a way to explore a more artistic side of my practice. Designing freely, without corporate goals or design systems getting in the way, and reigniting my passion for making, both physical and digital.

Interested in working together? Message me at mikael@wst.mn or connect on Linkedin.
