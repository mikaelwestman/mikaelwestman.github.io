Mikael Westman is product designer with 10+ years of experience in the creative and tech industry. He leads design & product initiatives from discovery to release and beyond.

He creates user experiences where form follows function, without losing sight of the lovable details that adds character to digital products; beautiful typography, icons, illustrations, and microinteractions.

Outside of work he often finds other creative outlets, such as music production, 3D modeling, painting, or cooking. And sometimes he just sit on the couch contemplating life while watching a football game.
