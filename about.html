Hi, I'm Mikael Westman, a product designer with 10+ years experience of shipping digital products and leading design from discovery to release, and beyond.

Good user experiences are built from the fundamentals: do the research, work iteratively, let form follow function. But in all that work I strive to not lose sight of the lovable details that adds character to digital products—beautiful typography, icons, illustrations, and microinteractions.

Outside of work I find energy in other creative outlets; music production, 3D modeling, painting, or cooking. And sometimes I just sit on the couch contemplating life while watching a football game.
