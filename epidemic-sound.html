Epidemic Sound is a music and sound effects library for storytellers working with TV & film, YouTube, live streams, podcasts, and other online content.

Driven by the increased demand for licensed music on online platforms, the company has gone through a rapid growth phase the last 3 years.

To keep our prominent position in an industry with increasing competition we've invested heavily into the core product. Improving the search & browse experience and building personalized recommendations to help storytellers find their next soundtrack easier.

On top of this, the product went through a major rebrand, which was made possible by the creation of a design system which I oversaw.
