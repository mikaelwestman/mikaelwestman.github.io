Epidemic Sound is a music and sound effects library for storytellers working with TV & film, YouTube, livestreams, podcasts, and other online content.

Driven by the increased demand for licensed music on online platforms, the company has gone through a rapid growth phase the last 3 years.

We've rebranded, established a design system, and most imporantly invested heavily into the core product to help storytellers find their next soundtrack with tools like search, browse, and personalized recommendations.
