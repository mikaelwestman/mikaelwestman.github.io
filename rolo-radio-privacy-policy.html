Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Overview

Rolo Radio is a radio streaming app. We respect your privacy and designed the app to collect as little data as possible. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No crash reports sent to third parties

Data Stored on Your Device

Rolo Radio stores your liked radio stations locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, this data syncs across your devices using Apple's iCloud service. This data never leaves Apple's ecosystem and is not accessible to us.

Music Identification

Rolo Radio uses Apple's ShazamKit to identify songs. When you use this feature, an audio fingerprint is processed locally on your device and matched against Apple's Shazam catalog. This is handled entirely by Apple's framework and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Network Requests

The app makes network requests solely to fetch radio station data and stream audio. No personal data is sent in these requests.

Third-Party Services

Rolo Radio does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any data from children or anyone else.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
