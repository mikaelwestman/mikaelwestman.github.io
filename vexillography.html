A study of the 5 principles for good flag design. All flags are based on fictional countries and regions from book, movies, or my own mind.

Inspired by the subreddit r/vexillology and this 99% Invisible episode.

Neo Hellas — A secular Greek state. The 2 circles represents the mainland and the disputed island of Cyprus.

Cherry Valley of Nangijala — The heavenly valley of after life from Astrid Lindgren's 'The Brothers Lionheart'.

Thorn Rose Valley of Nangijala — The tyrant region from 'The Brothers Lionheart'.

UCA, The United Coasts of America — A republic formed by the wealthy costal elites of the USA. Motto: "Divided by land, united in thought".

UFSA, The United Flyover States of America — The country passed over during transcontinental flights over America.

Eurasia — One of the superstates from George Orwell's '1984'.
