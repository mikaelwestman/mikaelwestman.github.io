Square for Restaurants is a point of sale system created to serve the specific needs of the hectic restaurant world.

After observing busy chefs, servers, and managers, who showed us archaic restaurant software that was incredibly feature rich but very complex to operate, we decided to build a reliable, self-serving point of sale system that let restaurant employees spend less time on technology and more time on what matters: providing tasty food and great service.

A glaring screen can ruin the vibe even in the coziest of bars. We created a dark mode that won't blind servers or guests.

Muscle memory is everything: experienced servers barely look at the screen while entering orders. To support this workflow we created generous tap targets and speedy transitions.

We removed duplicative work by integrating food delivery services with the point of sale, allowing servers to manage in-house and outside orders on the the same device.

See which tables are open, need attention, or are ready for the check. Behind the scenes information like guest count and restaurant performance is tracked automatically.

Track and edit all things related to the point of sale on Square Dashboard. Plenty of time was spent on updating the outdated navigation, which looked more like an internal Square org-chart than a tool for restaurants to manage day-to-day tasks.
